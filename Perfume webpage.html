About Our Boutique

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Essence & Elegance began as a small perfumery in Paris with a passion for creating unique, memorable fragrances. Our founder, Isabelle Laurent, a third-generation perfumer, wanted to bring the art of French perfumery to the world while maintaining the highest standards of craftsmanship.

Today, we operate boutiques in Paris, New York, and Tokyo, with our fragrances being shipped to over 50 countries worldwide. Despite our growth, we remain committed to our original values: quality, creativity, and personal service.

Our Craft

Each of our perfumes is created using traditional methods combined with modern innovation. We source the finest ingredients from around the world, including rare flowers from Grasse, spices from the Middle East, and exotic woods from Southeast Asia.

Our master perfumers undergo years of training to develop their olfactory skills. They carefully balance top, middle, and base notes to create fragrances that evolve beautifully throughout the day.

Our Team

Behind every bottle is a team of passionate individuals dedicated to the art of perfumery. From our master perfumers to our customer service representatives, each team member shares a deep appreciation for fragrance and a commitment to excellence.

We believe in continuous learning and regularly send our team to fragrance workshops and ingredient sourcing trips to ensure we stay at the forefront of the industry.
